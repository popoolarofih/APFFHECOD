Welcome to APFFHECOD

Association of Professional for Family Health Empowerment and Community Development (APFFHECOD) is a non-profit, non-partisan, non-religious, non-tribal and nongovernmental organization based in Auchi, Etsako West Local Government Area of Edo State. We are dedicated to promoting healthy families and communities.

About Us

Association of Professional for Family Health Empowerment and Community Development (APFFHECOD) was founded in 2011 by a group of concerned citizens who saw a need for a more holistic approach to family health. We believe that families are the foundation of strong communities, and that by empowering families to make healthy choices, we can create a healthier and more vibrant society. APFFHECOD engaged in community sensitization aimed at identifying problem and taking action to provide solutions to the problem.

  • Health education and promotion: We provide information and resources on a variety of health topics, such as nutrition, physical activity, mental health, and substance abuse.
  • Family support services: We offer support groups, counseling, and other services to help families cope with challenges such as poverty, violence, and chronic illness.
  • Community development: We work to build strong and healthy communities by providing resources and support to local organizations.

We believe that everyone deserves to live in a healthy and supportive environment. We are committed to working with families, communities, and policymakers to create a better future for all.

APFFHECOD is register with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC).

APFFHECOD

APFFHECOD is committed to promoting the health and well-being of families and communities. The organization believes that by working together, professionals can make a difference in the lives of families and communities. APFFHECOD is a valuable resource for professionals who work in the field of family health and community development. The organization offers a variety of programs and services that can help professionals improve their skills and knowledge, network with other professionals, and stay up-to-date on the latest research and policy developments. If you are a professional who works in the field of family health and community development, We encourage you to join APFFHECOD. The organization can help you make a difference in the lives of families and communities.
